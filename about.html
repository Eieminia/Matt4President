About Matthew Bonilla

Raised in the Chicago suburb Mount Prospect, Matthew Bonilla spent his high school academic career challenging himself in the world of STEM. Eventually graduating at the top of his class. However, despite his spectatcular self achievments he came to the cumclusion that he needed to help others and his community. It was then that he decided to run for class president, striving for a greater union between students, through events and activities, and an overwhelming Rose-Hulman spirit.
